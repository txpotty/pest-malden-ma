Itchy Bites and Skin Reactions

These bites can be mistaken for other insect bites, but their pattern and location can be indicative of bed bug activity. The bites themselves are a result of the bed bug feeding on your blood. Their saliva contains an anticoagulant and anesthetic, so you may not feel the bite happening. The itching and redness are allergic reactions to the saliva.
